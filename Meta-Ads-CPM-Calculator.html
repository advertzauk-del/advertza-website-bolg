What Drives Your CPM Up or Down

4 Causes of Above-Benchmark CPM

1. Low creative relevance score. Low-engagement creative signals poor audience fit, causing Meta to charge a higher CPM at auction. High-relevance creative earns CPM discounts. This is the most directly controllable CPM lever: and the first thing to check.

2. Audience overlap. Multiple ad sets targeting the same users forces your campaigns to bid against each other in the same auction. This self-competition inflates CPM across all overlapping ad sets simultaneously.

3. Placement inefficiency. Automatic placements may concentrate spend on expensive inventory. A single ad set running primarily on Instagram Feed while Reels and Stories remain underutilised will run above-benchmark CPM.

4. Account quality issues. Policy violations or high negative feedback rates permanently elevate CPM across all campaigns. Check Account Quality in Meta Business Suite if CPM is elevated across all campaigns regardless of creative.

7 Ways to Reduce Your Meta Ads CPM

  • Refresh creative: Launch 3–5 new ad variants prioritising Reels and UGC video: typically produces 15–25% lower CPM than static images within 7 days of launch
  • Consolidate campaigns: Merge overlapping ad sets into a single broad-targeting campaign to eliminate internal auction competition
  • Add audience exclusions: Exclude existing customers and recent website converters from prospecting to prevent wasted spend and CPM inflation
  • Activate Advantage+ Placements: Meta automatically routes budget toward lower-CPM inventory when creative quality supports it
  • Shift budget toward Reels and Stories: Both consistently produce below-average CPMs in UK and India in 2026
  • Implement CAPI: Recovers attributed conversions, improving algorithm signal and reducing optimisation CPM over time as the algorithm learns more efficiently
  • Time brand campaigns to Q1–Q2: 15–25% lower CPMs than Q4 in the UK market: awareness campaigns run most efficiently outside the Q4 retail peak

CPM is the first number I check in every account audit. If it is more than 20% above benchmark, the campaign is not a targeting problem or a budget problem: it is a creative problem or an account structure problem. Fix those, and CPM falls.

What is CPM in Meta Ads and how is it calculated?+
CPM stands for Cost Per Mille: the cost to deliver 1,000 impressions of your ad. The formula is: (Ad Spend ÷ Total Impressions) × 1,000. For example, if you spent £200 to achieve 20,000 impressions, your CPM is £10.00. CPM is the primary cost metric for Meta Ads because it reflects how much you pay for reach and is directly influenced by creative quality, audience competition, and placement selection.
What is a good CPM for Meta Ads in the UK in 2026?+
A good UK Meta Ads CPM varies by industry and placement. For Facebook Feed, below £9.00 is efficient and below £10.85 is average. For Instagram Feed, below £10.00 is efficient. For Reels, below £8.00. For Stories, below £6.00. By industry: ecommerce should target below £8.50, B2B below £10.00, restaurants below £7.50, and finance below £12.00. Any CPM more than 20% above the relevant benchmark warrants investigation.
What is a good CPM for Meta Ads in India in 2026?+
A good India CPM on Meta Ads is significantly lower than UK benchmarks due to lower advertising competition. For Facebook Feed, below ₹72 is efficient and below ₹85 is average. For Instagram Feed, below ₹88. For Reels, below ₹65. India CPMs vary by city: Mumbai and Delhi typically run 15–25% above the national average. Tier 2 and Tier 3 city targeting can produce CPMs 30–40% below the national average, making geographic segmentation within India a significant efficiency lever.
Why is my Meta Ads CPM above benchmark?+
Above-benchmark Meta Ads CPM is almost always caused by one of four issues: (1) Low creative relevance: low-engagement creative causes Meta to charge a premium for the same impression. (2) Audience overlap: multiple ad sets targeting the same users forces campaigns to bid against each other. (3) Placement inefficiency: spending concentrated on expensive inventory. (4) Account quality issues: policy violations or high negative feedback rates permanently elevate CPM. This calculator identifies whether CPM is above benchmark: the next step is diagnosing which of these causes applies.
How can I reduce my Meta Ads CPM quickly?+
The fastest CPM reduction strategies in order of impact: (1) Refresh creative: launch 3–5 new ad variants prioritising Reels and UGC video, typically producing 15–25% lower CPM within 7 days. (2) Consolidate audiences: merge overlapping ad sets into a single broad campaign to eliminate internal auction competition. (3) Review placement breakdown: exclude placements running 30%+ above target CPM. (4) Shift budget to Reels and Stories: both consistently produce below-average CPMs in both UK and India in 2026.
